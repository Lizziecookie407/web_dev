What even is Mistborn?

Mistborn is a stellar book series by Brandon Sanderson. There are three books in the first arc: The Final Empire, The Well of Assension, and The Hero of Ages. The second arc, known as the Wax and Wayne series, currently has four books: The Alloy of Law, Shadows of Self, The Bands of Mourning, and The Lost Metal. There is an addtional novalla called Secret History.

What makes Mistborn so good?

Mistborn is one of the most well thought out and intricate books I have ever read. The plot, characters, and twists are crafted in the most imersive way possible. The main character, Vin, is probably the most relatable character I have ever experienced being. You can tell only once you've reached the end that every single element was meticulously planned from the begining.

Magic system

A main part of the book is the magic system, called Allomancy. It is based on sixteen differnt types of metals. The idea is that each metal gives you a differnt power. These are a few of them.

  • Tin - enhanced senses
  • Pewter - enhanced strenth
  • Steel - the ability to allomantically push on mental
  • Iron - the ability to allomantically pull on mental
  • Zinc - the ability to pull, or riot, other's emotions
  • Brass - the ability to push, or soothe, other's emotions
