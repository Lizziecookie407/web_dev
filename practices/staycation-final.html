What the freak is it?

Capitol Reef is located in south-central Utah. it is known for its unique geology, rich cultural history, and spectacular dark night skies. It preserves the Waterpocket Fold, a nearly 100-mile-long geologic monocline, or "wrinkle" in the Earth's crust, that exposes layers of rock dating back millions of years. link
