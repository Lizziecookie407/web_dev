What even is Mistborn?

Mistborn is a stellar book series by Brandon Sanderson. There are three books in the first arc: The Final Empire, The Well of Assension, and The Hero of Ages. The second arc, known as the Wax and Wayne series, currently has four books: The Alloy of Law, Shadows of Self, The Bands of Mourning, and The Lost Metal.

What makes Mistborn so good?

Mistborn is one of the most well thought out and intricate books I have ever read. The plot, characters, and twists are crafted in the most imersive way possible. The main character, Vin, is probably the most relatable character I have ever experienced being. You can tell only once you've reached the end that every single element was meticulously planned from the begining.

What other books has Brandon Sanderson written?

Brandon Sanderson has written a whole slew of books and series. His biggest work is undiniably the Stormlight Archive, with 10 books planned out to have around 1000 pages each. Only five have been released so far. Another one of my favorites by him is the Skyward series, which has four escalating books.
